November 16, 1985

Glienicke Bridge, between West Berlin and East GermanyThe darkness seemed colder,
more bitter, at Glienicke Bridge when a spy exchange was about to begin.

Jay Tice shoved his hands deep into his topcoat pockets in a futile attempt
to warm them as he scanned the forested hills and the steel-and-iron bridge,
black and forbidding in the first rays of dawn.
